Taking the next step, for all mankind.

Since 1972, the world has waited for the next mission to explore the surface of our closest neighbour. It's been a long wait with many false starts and doomed ventures. Promises made and broken, dreams built and destroyed with endless red tape and beurocracy.

We're here to change that.

We've assembled a team of visionary leaders, industry titans and innovation experts to get boots in the dust within the next 5 years.

We're here to meet the challenge set in 1961.

When Kennedy set the goal of reaching the moon by the end of the decade, many thought his confidence was misguided; man was not meant for the stars.

He set the wheels in motion for those men and women who would work towards, and finally land on the moon in 1969, proving that it was not only possible, but that the human spirit longed to understand more of the world and the universe we live in.

PROJECT ATHENA is ready to take up the mantle and expand our understanding of the cosmos by establishing a permanent presence on the Moon.
